Frequently asked
Does Phoenix0 train on my resume?
No. Your inputs are scoped to your account and used only to generate your own drafts. We do not use your data to train shared models.
Which AI do you use?
A mix of frontier and open models, chosen per task. Drafting work runs on long-context models so the workspace's full history is in view; classification and matching run on smaller, faster ones. You can pick a research provider per session.
Can I export drafts to send through other tools?
Yes — every draft exports to PDF, DOCX, and plain text. The Career plan also outputs ATS-friendly resume formats.
Is there a Telegram bot?
Yes. The Telegram bot mirrors the web app's flows. The web app and the bot share a session, so you can start in one and finish in the other.
Will Phoenix0 apply to jobs for me automatically?
No. Applications that read the room are written, not blasted. Phoenix0 will surface matches and have drafts ready, but you press send.
